All for Comfortability

Founded in 1989, OWNDAYS aims to process a pair of quality glasses within 20 minutes from optometry to delivery. All glasses displayed in shop are original brands that are designed and manufactured by OWNDAYS that come with UV protection and dust-resistant coating.

The origin of the name of our company is a combination of “own days” and pronunciation of the word “on” as in on/off.We hope that we can make you feel refreshed day after day by wearing our glasses. In order to let all the people related to OWNDAYS as happy as possible, and getting even happier day by day, we will keep providing our best glasses.
